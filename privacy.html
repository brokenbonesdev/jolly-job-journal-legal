Privacy Policy

Jolly Job Journal Lite
Developed and published by Broken Bones LLC
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Overview

Jolly Job Journal Lite is a mobile application developed and published by Broken Bones LLC. The app is designed to help users track job applications in a simple, private, and offline-first manner.

Data Collection

Jolly Job Journal Lite does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

The app does not use:

  • User accounts or authentication
  • Analytics or telemetry
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party data services

Local Data Storage

All information entered into the app (such as company names, job titles, application statuses, and notes) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

Broken Bones LLC does not have access to this data.

Data Sharing

Jolly Job Journal Lite does not share data with third parties, does not transmit data off the device, and does not sell or monetize user data.

Tracking

This app does not track users across apps or websites. Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) is not applicable.

Children’s Privacy

Jolly Job Journal Lite is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly and the effective date will be revised.
